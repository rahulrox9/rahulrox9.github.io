Rahul Subbaraman

Volcanic petrologist finishing a PhD at the University of Manchester — I study the chemistry and textures of gabbroic nodules, rocks excavated from the Earth's crust by volcanic eruptions. Think of it as detective work: picking out clues from chemistry and texture, backed by rigorous quantitative analysis, microscopy, and a lot of Python and MATLAB.

Outside the lab I binge films and series, cook from whatever's in the pantry, travel whenever I can, and document it all — here, on Instagram, and occasionally in longer writing. This site is where I keep track of it all.
