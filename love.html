Love

Rahul Subbaraman · 14 February 2014

Love is not like weather,

That changes from time to time.

It should be the same forever,

It should be sweet and not like lime.

Love should be like our light,

Moves forward but never bends.

It binds us all close and tight,

So, love is one that never ends.

A true love not only gifts us with new friends,

But also makes everyone love us.

Loving and serving the humanity

Is equal to serving the almighty.
